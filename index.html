How to buy $KIOWA

1.

Download Your wallet

Download MetaMask  or TrustWallet (An app or wallet as we call it from Apple store og Google play). Now you need to add the Binance Smart Chain to your network-list. (Click here for a step-by-step tutorial).

2.

Buy and send BNB to MetaMask

Buy BNB on an exchange like Binance, Kraken, Coinbase etc. Transfer the tokens to your wallet address. BEP-20 addresses start with a "0x”.

3.

Go to pancakeswap and get KIOWA

Click here to head on over to PancakeSwap or use this address 0xBedC1BDF93eDC8cE9c91740a53c300646E45b5E5 to select kiowa. Set the slippage tolerance to 15% - 16% (sometimes it may be a bit more, depending on how much demand there is).

4.

Buy and send BNB to MetaMask

Swap BNB for KIOWA. Now you need to add KIOWA to your MetaMask (Tutorial here) or Trust Wallet to view your $KIOWA. Lastly HODL!
